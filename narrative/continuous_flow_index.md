## Anchor 1 – The creek moves quietly…
**File:** `anchor01.md`  
**Theme:** Smallness with dignity  
**Voice Layer:** Surface Water (quick, light)

**Moral Note:** Attention is a form of mercy; motion can be gentle without being weak.
