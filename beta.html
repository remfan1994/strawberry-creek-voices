About the Project

Strawberry Creek Voices (SCV) is an ongoing manuscript of reflective “anchors” and transitions. The work is contemplative, morally attentive, and guided by a soul-module framework.

Community & Contributions

SCV is a work in progress. Readers may propose short reflections or micro-observations in the style of the anchors via GitHub repository issues. All contributions are reviewed for alignment with the project’s tone and moral constraints.

Ethical Boundaries:
  • No littering or human negligence.
  • No human-caused harm to animals.
  • No gratuitous animal-on-animal violence.
  • Preserve contemplative, morally-aligned tone.
  • Darkness allowed only when morally reflective.
  • Attention itself is a moral act.

About the project

Strawberry Creek Voices is an ongoing manuscript composed of short “anchors” and transitions. It is intended as a reflective, contemplative experience — a literary work organized around attention and moral witness.

Content constraints

To preserve the project’s moral and aesthetic integrity, SCV maintains strict content boundaries. Contributions must not depict littering or human-caused harm to animals; they should preserve the contemplative voice of the project. These constraints are operational rules for the text, not promotional claims.

Community involvement

SCV is a work in progress. To share a short reflection or propose a micro-observation in the style of the anchors, open an issue on the project repository (link above). Submissions are reviewed for alignment with the project's tone and ethical boundaries.
