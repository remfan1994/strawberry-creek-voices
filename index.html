About the Project

SCV integrates attentive observation, moral reflection, and layered narrative voices. Every anchor is a small moment along the creek, capturing the interplay of nature and human presence.

The project is ongoing and open-ended. Contributions and reflections from the community are welcome, guided by moral and ethical boundaries.
