About

Strawberry Creek Voices (SCV) collects and shares the stories, memories, and music of our neighborhood. We create safe spaces for people to speak, record, and archive neighborhood life — centering youth, elders, and historically underrepresented voices.

Programs

  • Oral History Workshops — training sessions in interviewing and ethical archiving.
  • Community Listening Nights — shared listening events where we play locally contributed recordings and invite reflection.
  • Youth Story Labs — mentorship and media-skills sessions for young people.
  • Public Presentations — talks, readings, and site-based story installations (text & audio).

Upcoming events

Events are scheduled periodically. Join our mailing list or contact us for the latest calendar.

Get involved

  • Volunteer — help with workshops, transcription, or outreach.
  • Contribute a Story — we welcome recorded stories in any language; we offer editing support and release options.
  • Partner — if you're an organization interested in collaboration, we can design co-programming.

If you'd like to discuss a project, writing, or partnership, please reach out.
